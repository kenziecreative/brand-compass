**Defined:** 2026-03-26
**Core Value:** Discovery conversation produces brand outputs specific to the client, not generic templates

## Out of Scope

Explicitly excluded. Documented to prevent scope creep.

| Feature | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| Full design system methodology | Component-level tokens are design system work, not brand work (Pattern 23) |
| Full content strategy | Editorial calendars, SEO workflows, production processes are separate discipline (Pattern 18) |
| Enterprise governance | Full compliance infrastructure for large brand teams (Pattern 19) |
| Sonic identity | Relevant only for subset of clients, not universal enough (Pattern 34) |
| Full JTBD switch interviews | Research technique, not brand discovery framework (Pattern 2) |
| Custom iconography systems | Enterprise-tier only (Pattern 21) |
