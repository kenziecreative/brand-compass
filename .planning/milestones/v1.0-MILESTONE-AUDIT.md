### Voice Pipeline Break Detail (Warning, not Critical)

The Voice Analyzer writes `workspace/research/voice-fingerprint.md`. The Copywriter reads it as a voice seed. The Brand Verifier checks final outputs against it (Level 6, checks 6A-6D). However, the Document Assembler does not have voice-fingerprint.md in its input list.

**Impact:** Assembled prose may drift from the client's voice. The Brand Verifier catches this post-assembly, but adding the fingerprint to Document Assembler inputs would prevent drift rather than detect it.

**Affected requirements:** AGNT-02, QUAL-04 (both still marked satisfied — the safety net works, the pipeline just has a preventable gap).
